Who is Agnes Mar?

I’m a sophomore at the University of Michigan majoring in Information Analysis (Data Science and UX Design) and minoring in Gender, Race, and Nation. I'm from Palo Alto, California and Seattle, Washington. My friends would describe me as multifaceted, both academically and personally –– I love data and design, I’m analytical and creative, I’m incredibly hardworking but also prioritize my wellbeing. I’m passionate about utilizing my skills in data science and UX/UI design to create impactful experiences using empathy, intentionality, and creativity. In my free time, you can usually catch me watching the sunset or playing pickleball.

What I'm listening to: Mt. Joy, Noah Kahan, Dominic Fike

What I'm reading: Know My Name by Chanel Miller, Data Feminism by Catherine D'Ignazio and Lauren F. Klein, Invisible Women by Caroline Criado-Perez

What keeps me sane: The ocean, podcasts, journaling
